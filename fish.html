Fish Owners

  • ➤ Weekly water testing: pH, ammonia, nitrates, nitrites.
  • ➤ Freshwater Fish: pH 6.8-7.6; no ammonia.
  • ➤ Saltwater Fish: pH 7.6-8.4; salinity 1.020-1.028 sg.
  • ➤ Maintain toxin-free water and provide species-compatible environments.
  • ➤ Consult "Pexperts" for fish compatibility and care advice.
