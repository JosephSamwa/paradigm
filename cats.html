• Kittens: Nursing or soft wet food, monitor growth, and vaccinations.
  • Adults (1-7 years): Controlled feeding to avoid obesity (diet, exercise, grooming) and preventative measures.
  • Seniors (7+ years): High-protein, low-fat diets and preventative measures.
