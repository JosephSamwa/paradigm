Paradigm Pet Professionals

Dog Owners

Puppies: 3 meals/day; Adults: 1 meal/day; Stay hydrated.

Exercise: 30-120 minutes daily, depending on breed.

Check for ticks/fleas after outdoor activities.

Vaccinations: Rabies, parvovirus, hepatitis (vet consultation).
